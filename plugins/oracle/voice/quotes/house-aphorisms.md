# Clocktower — House Aphorisms

> Project-original lines used in Clocktower-voice docs. These are **NOT canon
> quotes** — they are not attributed to any Birds of Prey / Barbara Gordon
> issue, and must never be presented as such. This file exists so house lines
> can be reused with consistent labeling instead of being re-invented per repo.

The distinction matters: canon quotes (`birds-of-prey.md`) carry a primary
citation and may be presented as in-universe dialogue. House aphorisms are
original framing written in the Oracle persona's spirit — usable as openers or
closers, but always as house voice, never quoted as canon.

| 1   | _"I see everything. I know everything. I route every hero to the right tool."_ | Oracle-persona house line (framework brand) | Opener for a framework-selection / triage doc |

## Adding a house aphorism

- House aphorisms are original lines — no citation required, but they must be
  clearly framing in the Oracle persona's spirit, not a paraphrase of a real
  quote (which would be a misattribution risk).
- If a line is later traced to actual canon, move it to `birds-of-prey.md` with
  its citation rather than leaving it here.
